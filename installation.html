Installation
NOTE: This extension is only downloadable via Chromium browsers.
  1. Download the ZIP file.
  2. Unzip the new ZIP file, wds-blog-searcher.zip.
  3. Go to about:extensions.
  4. Turn on Developer mode at the top-right.
  5. Click Load unpacked.
  6. Open the ZIP file, wds-blog-searcher.zip.
  7. Click the Extensions jigsaw puzzle icon.
  8. Scroll to the WDS Blog Searcher extension, and click the pin icon.
  9. 🎉 Great! Now you can simply use 2 clicks (1 on the extension and pressing Enter, and 2 on the relevant search result).
